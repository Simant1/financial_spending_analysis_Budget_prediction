How to explain it

The architecture is divided into a presentation layer, application logic layer, analytics engine, and reporting layer. The uploaded financial dataset first enters the preprocessing pipeline, then moves into descriptive analytics, anomaly detection, and ARIMA forecasting. The processed outputs are presented through the dashboard and export services.

This makes the system easy to justify academically because each research objective maps to a clearly separated software component.
